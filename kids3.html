Planets of Our Solar System

Mercury Venus Earth Mars

Mercury

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun. It's also the smallest planet in our solar system.

Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun. It's similar in size to Earth but very hot!

Earth

Earth is our home! It's the only planet known to support life.

Mars

Mars is called the Red Planet. Scientists are exploring it for signs of life!
